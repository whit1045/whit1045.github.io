Coronavirus Update:

Creative Grapes will take all safety precautions to ensure staff and customers are safe. Health and safety protocols will be followed, with additional sanitation measures implemented. All high touch surfaces, including counters, debit machines, door handles, all bottling equipment, washing stations and corkers will be sanitized between all customers. Each bottling station will provide hand sanitizer. We plan to stagger bottlers to help maintain a safe physical distance between each other. Please feel free to contact us if you have any further questions regarding our safety protocols.
