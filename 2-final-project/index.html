About

Hey there, I’m DarkFire, I’m a 20 year old artist and aspiring content creator. I work mainly in traditional mediums for rough work and sketches, then switch to digital for clean up and colouring. I am excited to meet you and work together to create some art for whatever your needs. If you wish to commission me please contact me through my social media.

Contract & Pricing

All commissions are done under contract, you will receive watermarked update photos during the process, however the full, unmarked image will not be shared until full payment is received.

  • Full body: $30 CAD
  • 3/4 body: $25 CAD
  • Bust: $20 CAD
  • Headshot: $10 CAD

Rules

I will absolutely not tolerate any commissions that depict or promote any form of hate towards others. Please keep in mind that I specialize in character art for human/human shaped characters.
