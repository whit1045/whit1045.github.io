About Me

I'm 19 years old, and am a first year student at Algonquin College in the IMD. I enjoy drawing, reading, writing, and taking nature photos. I live in rural Ontario with my family, it's a very small town where everyone knows each other. I'm related to most of the people in the area as well, I have a big family and we enjoy spending time together.

I work at a library one town over from where I live, I work 10 hours a week, all year long. This is my 5th year working there, my job is to be there when the actual library is not open, so that the community has access to internet and the printer all week long.
